About

New to the world of technology, I decided to enroll at the College of Western Idaho for their Associate Degree in Software Development program.

I am in the beginning stages of my programming degree, so I have my primary experience in a more backend language, C#. My plan for future schooling is to enroll in more clubs and areas of participation, like their Cyber Security Club. Currently I am beginning to learn more frontend languages, such as HTML and CSS.

The change is a large one, but I know my experience working with different people to achieve desired outcomes was built in my previous industry.
